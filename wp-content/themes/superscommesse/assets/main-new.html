10 Reasons WhyYou Should Play Etheria
1.Voxel Graphics

Unique technology for working with voxels. We have gone from cubic worlds in favor of a beautiful atmospheric world with bright, good graphics.

2.Unique Mobs and Bosses

Unique, impossibly cute mobs, both peaceful and territorial that you’ll find on your journey. Oh, and the boss monsters are just as cute, if far more dangerous.

3. Non-target Combat System

Non-targeted battle system that prioritizes player’s skill over numerical advantages. Dodgeenemy’s attacks, and then bring down the pain!

4.The World Is Yours!

A procedure-generated huge world that a player can completely modify.

5.A Bunch of Characters

Five branches of the development of the game class for each character, each class has unique game mechanics

6. Voxel Construction

Build your home from hundreds of voxel species. Various architectural elements will make your home unique. Develop it, decorate and invite friends.

7.Combat Construction

Use blueprints to build walls of stairs and defensive buildings during the battle.

8.Polymorph system

The ability to turn into mobs and use their abilities.

9.Guild System

Guild system, team up with friends and build your city.

10.The System of Battlegrounds

Community events to shake things up, up to, and including, Battlegrounds where the last adventurer standing wins
